The Code

FLEXIT is more than a gym; it's a high-performance laboratory. Our policies ensure that every athlete can pursue excellence without distraction or compromise.

Gym Etiquette

fitness_center

Re-Rack Your Weights

Respect the lab. Always return weights, bars, and plates to their designated storage after use. Leaving equipment out is an admission of weakness.

cleaning_services

Sanitize Station

Wipe down all surfaces post-set. Cleanliness is a mandatory standard here.

timer

Respect the Clock

Limit rest periods on peak machines. Don't scroll while others wait to work in.

volume_off

No Loud Speakers

Use headphones. Your playlist isn't global. Respect the mental focus of other athletes.

The Zero-Ego Zone

Dropping weights aggressively or screaming during sets is permitted only in the Power Cage area. Keep the main floor professional and controlled. We value technique over noise.

Safety Guidelines

  • check_circle

    Footwear Protocol

    Closed-toe athletic shoes must be worn at all times. No sandals, boots, or bare feet allowed on the training floor.

  • check_circle

    Spotting & Safety Bars

    Always use safety pins in power racks. Ask for a spot when lifting heavy. No solo maxing without spotters.

  • check_circle

    Incident Reporting

    Report any equipment malfunction or injury immediately to floor staff. Zero tolerance for compromised safety.

Membership Terms

Cancellations

All membership cancellations require a 30-day written notice. No prorated refunds for mid-month cancellations. Administrative fees may apply to contract breaks.

Access Control

Your digital key is strictly for individual use. Granting access to non-members will result in immediate termination of membership without refund.

Guest Policy

Guests must sign a liability waiver and pay the daily drop-in fee. One guest per member per visit is permitted during staffed hours.

Age Requirements

Members must be 18+ years of age. Minors (16-17) require a legal guardian's signature and must complete a mandatory orientation session.
